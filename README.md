## üìñ Learning Journey & Methodology

### 1. The CIA Triad
Researching the bedrock of information security:
* **Confidentiality:** Ensuring data is accessible only to authorized users (e.g., End-to-end encryption in WhatsApp).
* **Integrity:** Maintaining the accuracy and consistency of data (e.g., Preventing unauthorized balance changes in banking apps).
* **Availability:** Ensuring systems are up and running when needed (e.g., Defending against DDoS attacks on social media platforms).

### 2. Threat Actor Profiling
Identifying who launches attacks and their motivations:
* **Script Kiddies:** Unskilled individuals using pre-made tools.
* **Insiders:** Employees or contractors with authorized access who misuse it.
* **Hacktivists:** Actors driven by political or social causes.
* **Nation-State Actors:** Highly sophisticated, government-sponsored groups.

### 3. Attack Surface Exploration
Mapping out the "doors and windows" of an organization:
* **Web & Mobile Apps:** User interfaces and client-side vulnerabilities.
* **APIs:** The bridges between different software components.
* **Network & Cloud:** Infrastructure, servers, and virtualized environments.

### 4. Vulnerability Research
Deep dive into the **OWASP Top 10** to understand critical web application risks, such as Broken Access Control, Cryptographic Failures, and Injection.
